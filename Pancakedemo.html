Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 & half cups all-purpose flour
  2. baking powder
  3. teaspoon of salt
  4. white sugar
  5. cup of milk
  6. egg

Steps

Steps 1

in a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter mix until smooth

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
